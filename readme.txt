The WP-Tipbot is an easy to setup WordPress plugin to get XRP tips for your content. Displays the XRP TIP BOT button with a widget or shortcode.

== Description ==

As content author youâ€™re spending hours and days creating great blog articles and pages for the community. And you have the right to ask for a tip. Even the smallest tip will count but whet it comes to transactions and bank taxes things get ugly. But not in the world of the blockchain and the cryptocurrency.

Thanks to Wietse Wind and his [XRP Tip Bot](https://www.xrptipbot.com/howto) you can send and recieve Ripple XRP tips - easily and fast. And now we have have created an easy way for you to integrate the XRP Tip Bot in WordPress as a widget in your sidebar or as shortcode on any page you would like.

This plugin will give you the option to creat a XRP tip button with your own settings like ammount of the tip, and thank you message.
